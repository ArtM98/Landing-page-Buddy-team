Our Mission

+Buddy as born out of a mission to help people connect ‘genuinely’ with each other and help themselves managing their emotions better with a steady progress everyday.

We aim to be the ‘social app’ that connects people and brings them closer together, in this divided, fast changing world.

Product

+Buddy is offering three complementary features to help studends better manage their emotions and overcome their everyday challenges.

  • +Check-in

    • Daily check-in, you will be able to have a discussion about your day with a random and anonymous partner. The goal is to express yourself and take step back from the day you just finished.

  • +Discussions

    • More than everyday discussions,these are special tracks designed by professional therapists to help you overcome specific challenges such as: stress, workload management or situtional anxiety.

  • +Games

    • Interactive activities that have one goal: help you on the short and long term. Medidation has proven to be benefic in multiple situations.
